About

My research interests are in exploring latent characteristic of people based on machine learning models. In particular, I study developing deep neural network models for computational psychotherapy. Currently, I collaborate with Trost, in order to develop conversational AI agents for psychotherapy which help clients describe their problems more throughly, as well as counselors conduct sessions effeciently.
